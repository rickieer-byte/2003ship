Flow 1

Standard End-to-End Container Delivery Pipeline

Demonstrates the core operational workflow: dispatching an arrived container, driver portal authentication, prime mover slot allocation at the port gate, geofenced port pickup, geofenced warehouse arrival, and POD signing.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click the Setup button to log in as dispatcher_user.
  2. On the dashboard, click Instant Allocate next to container NYKU9012455. Notice it assigns driver Siti Aisha.
  3. Open the Driver App (link in nav) in a private window or another tab, log in as Siti Aisha (phone tail 5432).
  4. Accept the job, and click Book Gate Slot. Notice it books port slot #1.
  5. Simulate geofence updates on the tracking map or let the simulation ticks complete.
Flow 2

Driver Job Rejection & Re-allocation Cooldown

Verifies driver autonomy and the system's re-allocation rules. A driver rejects an assigned job, triggering a cooldown blackout period and resetting the container to Pending.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup to auto-assign a container to driver Mubarak Ali and log you into the driver portal.
  2. On the portal, click Decline Job, enter a rejection reason (e.g., "Engine issues"), and submit.
  3. Go back to the Operations Hub. Observe that the container has reset to Pending, displaying a warning note: "Mubarak Ali declined (Engine issues)".
  4. Click Instant Allocate again. Mubarak Ali is in cooldown, so the algorithm bypasses him and selects the next nearest available driver.
Flow 3

DSS Warnings for Exhausted Fleet Resources

Demonstrates the Decision Support System (DSS) alert modal. When no internal fleet drivers are on shift or available, the system computes projected storage and demurrage charges to guide the user on emergency hires.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup to set all internal drivers to Offline and log you in as a Dispatcher.
  2. Click Instant Allocate next to container NYKU9012455.
  3. Observe the DSS modal presenting the port charges projection, emergency hire comparison, and a recommended strategic action (PROCEED or Defer).
Flow 4

Staff Leave Application (Within Limits)

Registers a standard staff leave request for a driver, dispatcher, or planner. The system validates the request against active schedules and approved leave dates.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup to navigate directly to the Leave Management panel as Fleet Manager.
  2. Select employee type Driver and select driver Sarah Tan.
  3. Choose a future date (e.g., tomorrow) and click Submit Request.
  4. Notice that the leave request is successfully recorded in the table list.
Flow 5

SQL Trigger-based Leave Constraint Validation

Tests the strict corporate limit of maximum 2 people per employee type on leave on any single day. This validation is enforced at the database level by SQL Triggers before record insertion.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup. This pre-seeds 2 driver leave requests for tomorrow and logs you in as Fleet Manager.
  2. Try to submit a new leave request for a third driver (e.g. Marcus Chen) on the exact same date (tomorrow).
  3. Observe the database-level error caught and returned in a flash message: "Leave limit reached: A maximum of 2 employees of this type can be on leave on any given day."
Flow 6

Leave Integration with Driver Dispatch Pool

Demonstrates how an approved leave request dynamically overrides driver roster status, marking them unavailable for auto-allocation rules.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup. This registers driver Siti Aisha as on leave today and logs you in as a Dispatcher.
  2. Click Drivers in the nav bar. Observe that Siti Aisha is listed as On Leave (instead of Available).
  3. Return to the hub and attempt to dispatch container NYKU9012455. Notice that Siti Aisha is bypassed, and the system chooses the next available driver.
Flow 7

Shift Schedule Roster & Profile Updates (CRUD)

Demonstrates full CRUD operations on logistics assets. The Fleet Manager registers new drivers, amends phone details/statuses, and adjusts their weekly 12-hour staggered schedules.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup to log in as Fleet Manager and open the Roster Manager dashboard (`/fleet`).
  2. Scroll down and create a new driver (e.g., name "Ali Hasan", phone "+65 8234 5678"). Click Add.
  3. Update a driver's phone number or set their status to Offline.
  4. Change weekly shift timings for Marcus Chen. Confirm changes are updated.

Flow 10

Demurrage Analytics & Financial Exports

Calculates and reports financial savings from avoiding demurrage and storage rent penalties. The Fleet Manager can export the findings to a zipped report containing CSV files.

Step-by-Step Instructions:
  1. Click Setup to pre-seed completed deliveries that incurred demurrage/rent and open `/analytics`.
  2. Observe the Carrier Benchmark table showing calculated fines and port rent leakage.
  3. Click Export Analytics CSV Report. Observe a ZIP file download containing the processed reports.
